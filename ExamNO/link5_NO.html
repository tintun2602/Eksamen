Our Prospects for RE-SORT

What are our goals? What purpose does Re-sort serve? Naturally our goal is to facilitate the disposal of trash, while also facilitating the recycling and re-using of materials from garbage waste. Us humans consume and throw a lot of products on a daily basis. Normally in a year, we dump over 2.0 billion tons of waste, most of it coming from newly bought products we trash within the first 6 months of use. That is a lot of waste, waste we must properly collect, sort and recycle if we want to cut down our global wastes. Re-sort will be our attempt to tackle this problem. Not only that, but spread awareness over how much of our waste goes unsorted, and the importance of recycling. The process of creating a cleaner and less wasteful society is a challenging journey, but this is one of the steps towards that ultimate goal: an enviromentally friendly world.

Why recycle?

Recycling is the process of converting waste materials into new materials and objects. The recyclabiltiy of a material depends on its ability to reaquire the properties it had in its virgin or original state. It is an alternative to "conventional" waste disposal that can save material and help lower greenhouse gas emissions.


The effects of sorting and recycling

  • Sorting facilitates recycling waste.
  • Recycling waste allows for reusing its materials.
  • Reusing materials cuts down new material production.
  • The processing of raw materials also becomes less frequent.
  • Reducing the factory works of production and processing directly cuts own the greenhouse gas emissions.
